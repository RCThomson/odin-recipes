Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Here is my stepmother's delicious chocolate chip cookie recipe! They taste best with big chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (12 oz) chunky chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. With electric mixer, cream butter and shortening. Add vanilla and sugars. Cream well. Add eggs. Mix.
  2. Add dry ingredients until thoroughly mixed. Fold in chips. Refrigerate dough about 15 minutes.
  3. Drop by teaspoons onto greased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes. If you want, bake them for 7 minutes on lower rack and move them to upper rack for 2 minutes.
