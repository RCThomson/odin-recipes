Strawberry Salad

Description

Here is a side dish that's popular in my family, especially around Thanksgiving. Despite the name, it's more of a dessert than a salad, almost like a sweet casserole.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups coarsely crushed pretzels
  • 3/4 cup melted margarine
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 8 oz. cream cheese (soft)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups whipped topping
  • 1 6-oz. strawberry jello
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 10-oz. packages frozen strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine crushed pretzels, melted butter & 3 tablespoons sugar.
  2. Press mixture into 10 x 15 inch ungreased pan or baking dish.
  3. Bake 10 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Cool.
  4. Soften cream cheese and mix sugar in thoroughly. Fold whipped topping into cream cheese mixture. Spread over preztel mix. Chill.
  5. Dissolve strawberry gelatin into boiling water. Add frozen strawberries and stir until melted. Chill.
  6. When gelatin is almost set, pour over cream cheese layer. Chill.
